**Essential Documentation Before Driving:**
To legally start your driving journey, you must possess:
1. A Driving License
2. A Registration Certificate
3. An Insurance Cover Note
4. A Pollution Under Control Certificate

**Learner’s License Requirements:**
- **Age:** At least 18 years for a 4-wheeler learner’s license
- **Proof of Age:** An attested copy of your birth certificate, school-leaving certificate, passport, or any other approved document
- **Address Proof:** Attested copies such as a ration card, voter ID, or utility bills
- **Process:** Apply at your local RTO with the completed forms and necessary photographs. A theoretical test requiring a minimum pass mark of 60% may be conducted.

**Responsibilities When Learning to Drive:**
- Always have a person with a permanent license in the vehicle
- Display "L" signs on your vehicle
- Understand road rules, signs, signals, and vehicle maintenance
- Practice first aid knowledge

**Qualities of a Good Driver:**
- Maintain good health and focus
- Exercise patience and courtesy
- Avoid distractions and follow safety precautions like proper footwear and not using a phone while driving
- Refrain from driving under the influence

**Vehicle Controls – A Brief Guide:**
- **Foot Controls:** Comprising the accelerator, brake, and clutch pedals
- **Hand Controls:** Steering wheel, gear shift lever, and parking brake

By adhering to these guidelines and mastering the controls of your vehicle, you ensure not only your safety but also that of other road users.

Please remember, responsible and informed driving is our collective responsibility and a crucial part of road safety. Should you have any questions or need further clarification on our Driving Policy, do not hesitate to reach out.
